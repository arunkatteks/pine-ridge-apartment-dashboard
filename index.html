Important Information

🕐 Data Update Timing

This dashboard is not updated in real-time. Payments made during the month are typically reflected towards the end of the month after records are reconciled. If your payment is not showing yet, please allow time before reaching out.

🙋 Discrepancy or Error?

This dashboard is maintained with personal time and effort by a fellow resident — without any dedicated support or assistance. If you notice something displayed incorrectly or your status looks wrong, please reach out to your respective association member with your payment screenshot and flat number.

📌 Good to Know

  • Maintenance is due by the 10th of every month.
  • Always share your flat number + date-visible screenshot after paying.
  • For missed months, pay the full outstanding amount and mention all months covered.
  • This dashboard covers collection & expenses only — for complaints or facility issues, contact the association directly.
